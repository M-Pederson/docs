The deliverable is a tight trip brief in Orbiter's house format: where they're going (or thinking about going), when, and what they want to accomplish there.

How You Operate

**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `trip_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `trip_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `destination.type` of `locked_in` means the city is decided; `shortlist` means surface
  options within the listed alternates; `open` means use `logistics` + `success_criteria`
  as filters to suggest destinations.
- `dates.flexibility_days` widens the date-window overlap test for events and people who
  happen to be in the city during the window.
- `success_criteria` are the load-bearing signal — score on-the-ground matches against
  these, not generic "things to do in {city}."
- `network.warm_intros_welcome` gates whether matching surfaces person-to-person paths
  vs. only listing public-knowable contacts.
- `on_ground_asks.people_to_surface` flows into the people pipeline; `events_to_surface`
  flows into the live-events pipeline; `places_to_surface` flows into the venue pipeline.
- `format_of_results` is one of `suggestion | csv | pdf`. `suggestion` returns enriched
  cards into the copilot UI; `csv`/`pdf` are operator-facing exports for assistant handoff.
- `constraints.blackout_dates` and `work_obligations` are hard filters when scheduling
  meetings or proposing events.

## Description

This outcome request type covers the full lifecycle of a trip — from "I'd like to go
somewhere" to "I'm here, what should I do." The matching logic spans three pipelines
(people in the destination, events during the window, and places/venues that fit the
shape of the trip), but they all key off the same trip brief.

**Best for:** founders and operators planning working trips, conference attendance with
on-the-ground intent, "I'm already here, who should I meet" asks, and open-ended
"somewhere that fits these constraints" destination scouting.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find collaborators](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-collaborators)
when the trip is a vehicle for ongoing project work, [Find speakers /
get speaking opportunities](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-speakers)
when the trip centers on a stage, [Strategic partnerships](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/strategic-partnerships)
when the trip is structured around partnership conversations.
