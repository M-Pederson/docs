**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `advice_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `advice_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `must_haves` are hard filters — a candidate missing any one is excluded. `nice_to_haves` are
  ranking signals only.
- `depth.ongoing_relationship: false` signals this is a one-off connection, not an advisor seat.
  Prioritize people who are available for paid single consultations, not just ongoing engagements.
- `trust_signal_required.type: "credentialed_professional"` is a strong filter — run credential
  verification against profile data before surfacing. Exclude anyone whose highest credential is
  a non-clinical coaching certification.
- `deal_breakers` include category-level exclusions (influencer, MLM, supplement brand) — apply
  these as domain-tag filters, not just name-match filters.
- `exclusion_list.categories` is a freeform list of negative categories; parse and map to
  internal domain taxonomy before applying.

## Description

This outcome type is the catch-all for off-pattern requests that don't fit cleanly into the other 13. The User has a specific question or situation and needs to find someone with relevant expertise — usually a one-time conversation, not an ongoing relationship. Personal asks ("I want to lose 20 pounds") and tactical professional questions ("I need an M&A tax attorney") both land here when no other outcome type fits.

**Best for:** off-pattern user asks, niche expert one-off conversations, personal/lifestyle questions outside the standard business outcomes.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find a mentor / advisor](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-mentor-advisor) when the relationship is ongoing instead of one-off, [Make a purchase](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/make-a-purchase) when the relationship is a paid service rather than a conversation.
