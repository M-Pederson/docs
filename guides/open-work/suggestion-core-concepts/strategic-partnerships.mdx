## Description

This outcome type surfaces companies (and the right person inside them) for partnership-level relationships — co-marketing, integrations, co-selling, channel deals, or joint go-to-market. The relationship is partner-to-partner, not vendor-to-customer or investor-to-portfolio.

**Best for:** BD/partnerships leads, founder-led channel building, ecosystem teams, joint-venture scoping.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Prospect for customers / clients](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/prospect-customers-clients) when selling to instead of partnering with, [Find acquisition targets](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-acquisition-target) when M&A is on the table.
