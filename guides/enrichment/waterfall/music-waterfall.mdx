MusicBrainz enrichment waterfall — a rate-limited (1 req/s), depth-capped cascade that pulls artists, groups, labels, recordings, release-groups, releases, works, events, and places into the Orbiter graph, with identity bridges to `master_person` and `Company`. This is the group overview; the table schemas, node/edge references, and function inventory live on the companion pages below.
