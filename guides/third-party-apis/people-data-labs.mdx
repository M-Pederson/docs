## What it is

[People Data Labs](https://www.peopledatalabs.com) (PDL) is a B2B data provider offering person and company enrichment APIs. Given an email, name, or LinkedIn URL, PDL returns structured professional data — job history, education, skills, social profiles, and company details.

## Why we use it

- **Person enrichment** — job titles, employers, education, skills, social links from name/email/LinkedIn
- **Company enrichment** — industry, size, location, funding, tech stack, alternative domains
- **High coverage** — 1.5B+ person profiles, 100M+ company records
- **Structured output** — clean JSON with normalized fields ready for graph ingestion

## How we use it at Orbiter

PDL is a primary data source in the **person** enrichment pipeline and a create-time data source for the **company** pipeline. Raw PDL responses are stored in `person_enrich_data.people_data_labs` and `company_enrich_data.people_data_labs`. Person-side, PDL is processed by `process-person-phase-3` #12824 (skills, work history, education, social links, bios). Company-side, PDL data is processed inline by `mvp/get-add/master-company-new` #12558 at initial create time (industries, addresses, links, about text, alternative domains).

<Note>
As of `run-base-company-enrich-v3` v3.5 (2026-05-11), PDL is **no longer called or processed by the post-create company orchestrator**. The orchestrator was switched to an Exa-driven flow (`get-exa-company-c-suite` #12988) for founder + key-employee enrichment. Company-side PDL processing now happens exclusively in `get-add/master-company-new` at create time. PDL remains active in the person enrichment pipeline.
</Note>

Key data extracted:
- **Person:** job history, education, skills, social URLs, location
- **Company:** industry, tags, summary, headquarters address, social URLs, founded year, ticker, alternative names/domains
