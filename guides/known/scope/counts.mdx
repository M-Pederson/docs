---
title: "Interaction Counts"
description: "Cron jobs that maintain interaction count fields (lifetime + rolling windows) on KNOWS edges"
---

Interaction counts on `KNOWS` edges are cron-populated. Raw logs (email, calendar, meetings, phone, chat, social) are the source of truth; `KNOWS` holds the rollup. Each modality has four count fields: lifetime plus 30d, 90d, and 365d rolling windows.

## Why time-bucketed?

Lifetime totals alone make scoring brittle. A relationship with 800 emails in 2015–2018 and zero since should score very differently from a relationship with 800 emails in the last 12 months. Rolling windows let the scoring cron answer "is this relationship *currently* active" without scanning raw logs.

See [Documentation — Time granularity decision](/guides/known/documentation#scoring-model) for the rationale.

---

## One cron per modality

Separate cron jobs so modalities can run at different frequencies. Email is high-volume → every 15 minutes. Phone is lower-volume → hourly.

### `known/counts/update_email_counts` (cron)

Scans email activity logs and refreshes `total_email_lifetime`, `total_email_last_30d`, `total_email_last_90d`, `total_email_last_365d` on every `KNOWS` edge with email evidence. Updates `last_direct_interaction_at` if newer emails are found. Stamps `last_counts_updated_at`.

**Cadence:** Every 15 minutes

### `known/counts/update_calendar_counts` (cron)

Refreshes `total_shared_calendar_events_*` fields.

**Cadence:** Every 30 minutes

### `known/counts/update_meeting_counts` (cron)

Refreshes `total_remote_meetings_*` and `total_irl_meetings_*` fields (distinguished by event location data).

**Cadence:** Every 30 minutes

### `known/counts/update_phone_counts` (cron)

Refreshes `total_phone_calls_*` fields.

**Cadence:** Hourly

### `known/counts/update_chat_counts` (cron)

Refreshes `total_chat_messages_*` fields (SMS, iMessage, Slack DMs, etc.).

**Cadence:** Every 15 minutes

### `known/counts/update_social_counts` (cron)

Refreshes `total_social_interactions_*` fields (likes, comments, replies on social platforms).

**Cadence:** Hourly

---

## Orchestrator

### `known/counts/update_all_counts`

Sequences all per-modality count updates for a given `KNOWS` edge or batch. Used by the [scoring cron](/guides/known/scope/scoring) as a dependency: scoring cannot run until counts are fresh.

**Inputs:** `knows_edge_uuid` OR `batch_uuids[]`
**Outputs:** `{counts_updated_at: timestamp}`

---

## Implementation notes

- **Differential updates:** Each count cron should use `last_counts_updated_at` on KNOWS as its "high water mark" — only process edges touched since that timestamp, not scan the whole graph.
- **Rolling windows are recomputed, not incremented.** The 30d window isn't "add this week's count minus last week's" — it's "recount the last 30 days from raw logs." This is simpler and avoids drift.
- **Evidence coverage matters.** If only one person's email is imported (see [evidence coverage](/guides/known/schema-design#evidence-coverage)), counts are asymmetric by design. The scoring cron handles this via `adjusted_reciprocity`.
- **Stamp `last_direct_interaction_at`** from the latest timestamp observed across all modalities, not just the one this cron is updating.
