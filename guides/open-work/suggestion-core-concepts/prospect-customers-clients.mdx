## Description

This outcome type surfaces specific people who match the User's ideal customer profile (ICP) — decision-makers, end users, or influencers at companies that should buy what the User sells. The goal is warm-target prospecting, not generic lead lists.

**Best for:** B2B sales, agency/services prospecting, high-touch B2C, nonprofit fundraising.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Identify strategic partnerships](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/strategic-partnerships) for partner relationships (not buyer), [Find investors](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-investors) when raising capital instead of selling product.
