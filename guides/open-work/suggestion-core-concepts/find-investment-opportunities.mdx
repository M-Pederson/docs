**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `opportunity_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `opportunity_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `must_haves` are hard filters — a company failing any one is excluded from results.
  `nice_to_haves` are ranking signals only.
- `sector_thesis.adjacent_excluded` is a strong exclusion signal — companies matching only
  excluded adjacencies should be penalized, not returned as near-matches.
- `founder_profile.background_required` is the highest-recall discriminator for first-pass
  filtering; use it before sector scoring when possible.
- `source_preference.banker_process_ok: false` means companies currently on a sell-side
  mandate should be excluded even if they match the thesis.
- `format_of_results` is one of `suggestion | csv | pdf`. `suggestion` returns enriched
  cards into the copilot UI; `csv`/`pdf` are operator-facing exports for IC memo or LP reporting.
- `exclusion_list.master_person_ids` is populated when the user has already passed on
  founders or companies ("don't show me anyone I've already reviewed").

## Description

This outcome type surfaces companies, deals, or assets for the User to invest in. The User is deploying capital — angel checks, VC follow-ons, family-office allocations, or PE/M&A platform plays — and wants targets that match a thesis.

**Best for:** angel investors, VC sourcing, PE/family-office deal flow, fund-of-funds allocators.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find investors](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-investors) for the inverse (raising capital), [Find acquisition targets](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-acquisition-target) for control deals instead of minority.
