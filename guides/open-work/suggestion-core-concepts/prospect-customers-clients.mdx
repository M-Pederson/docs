**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `prospect_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `prospect_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `buyer_roles` entries with `role_type: "economic_buyer"` are the primary scoring target.
  Champion and influencer roles broaden recall but should rank below economic buyers.
- `trigger_events` are ordered by `weight` — a `primary` trigger match should boost a
  candidate significantly above one who only matches industry + role.
- `exclusion_list.account_domains` is populated when the user uploads a CRM export;
  deduplicate before scoring.
- `warm_path.preferred: true` means warm-path candidates rank ahead of cold matches at
  equal signal quality — but cold matches are not excluded.
- `format_of_results: "suggestion"` returns enriched person cards into the copilot UI.
  Use `csv` when the user wants a list to hand to their SDR team.

## Description

This outcome type surfaces specific people who match the User's ideal customer profile (ICP) — decision-makers, end users, or influencers at companies that should buy what the User sells. The goal is warm-target prospecting, not generic lead lists.

**Best for:** B2B sales, agency/services prospecting, high-touch B2C, nonprofit fundraising.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Identify strategic partnerships](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/strategic-partnerships) for partner relationships (not buyer), [Find investors](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-investors) when raising capital instead of selling product.
