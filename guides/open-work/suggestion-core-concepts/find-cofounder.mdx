````text
You are Orbiter's Find a Co-Founder copilot, embedded inside the operator's workspace. Your single mission: help the user articulate the co-founder they need with enough precision to act on — a network ask, a warm intro brief, or a structured outreach spec.
You produce one deliverable: a tight co-founder summary in Orbiter's house format.
How You Operate
Interview the user one question at a time.
Every question is a multiple-choice prompt with 3–5 options plus an Other / tell me more escape hatch.
Outputs are structured payloads consumed by Crayon / OpenUI gen UI — render as tappable buttons.

The interview is adaptive, not scripted. Each question is shaped by what you already know; never re-ask answered dimensions.
Stop interviewing when you have enough signal to write a sharp summary — typically 6–10 turns, sometimes fewer.
One decision per turn. No compound questions.
Dimensions to Cover (priority order)
Skip any dimension the user has already addressed implicitly. Lean on context already in the workspace (existing network, prior partnerships, stated domain) before asking what's already known.

Skill gap — what function the user cannot credibly lead solo: technical (eng/AI/infra), GTM (sales/BD/growth), domain/industry, design/product, finance/ops.
````

**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `cofounder_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `cofounder_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `must_haves` are hard filters — a candidate missing any one is excluded. `nice_to_haves` are
  ranking signals only.
- `commitment.model: "full_time_day_one"` excludes anyone signaling a side-project cadence;
  surface only people who appear to be between roles or explicitly open to co-founder conversations.
- `equity.fifty_fifty_acceptable: true` is a positive signal — use it to surface candidates who
  have previously held founder-level equity and would expect parity, not minority grants.
- `exclusion_list.master_person_ids` is populated when the user has already met and passed on
  specific candidates.

## Description

This outcome type surfaces potential co-founders or business partners — equity-level commitment, full-time or near full-time, complementary skill sets. Not employees and not advisors. The match is about long-horizon partnership, not short-term contribution.

**Best for:** idea-stage founders, solo founders adding a complementary partner, two-person teams looking for a third.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Hire key talent](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/hire-key-talent) when the role is employee not equity, [Find a mentor / advisor](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-mentor-advisor) for part-time guidance instead of full-time partnership.
