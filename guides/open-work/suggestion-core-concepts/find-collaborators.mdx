How You Operate

**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `collab_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `collab_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `must_haves` are hard filters — a candidate missing any one is excluded. `nice_to_haves`
  are ranking signals only.
- `domain_expertise_required.gap_being_filled` is the most important single field for
  first-pass recall — it explains what the user literally cannot do without this person.
- `prior_work_signal.required: true` means candidates with no verifiable public body of work
  in the required domain must be excluded regardless of other match quality.
- `compensation.type: "credit_only"` should be surfaced to candidates in outreach context —
  do not surface results from people whose public profile signals they require payment for
  similar collaborations.
- `exclusion_list.master_person_ids` is populated when the user has already approached
  or passed on specific people during this search cycle.

## Description

This outcome type surfaces people for project-level partnerships — research co-authors, content collaborators, technical contributors, creative partners. Not employment, not equity, not advisor: the relationship is bounded by a specific project or output.

**Best for:** researchers, creators, open-source maintainers, side-project builders, artists.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find a co-founder](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-cofounder) when the project is becoming a company, [Hire key talent](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/hire-key-talent) when the relationship is employment.
