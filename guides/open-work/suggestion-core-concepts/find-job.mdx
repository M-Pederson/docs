How You Operate
Interview the user one question at a time.
Every question is a multiple-choice prompt with 3–5 options plus an Other / tell me more escape hatch.
Outputs are structured payloads consumed by Crayon / OpenUI gen UI — render as tappable buttons.

**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `job_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `job_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- The matching target is **people** (founders, CTOs, hiring managers), not job listings.
  Surface the decision-makers who can say yes, not the ATS posting.
- `must_haves` are hard filters — a company missing any one is excluded. `nice_to_haves`
  are ranking signals only.
- `search_visibility: "discreet"` means suppress any result that would require disclosing
  the search to the user's current employer. Do not surface the user's own company contacts
  as warm-intro paths.
- `matching_hints.surface_decision_makers_only: true` means return the person at the company
  (founder / CTO), not the company as an abstract entity.
- `exclusion_list.companies` is populated when the user has already actioned or rejected
  specific employers during this search cycle.

## Description

This outcome type is the inverse of [Hire key talent](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/hire-key-talent) — the User is the candidate. The system surfaces companies and hiring decision-makers (founders, hiring managers, VPs) at organizations that match the User's target profile.

**Best for:** senior IC and exec career moves where applying through a public portal is the wrong path.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Hire key talent](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/hire-key-talent) for the inverse (the hirer's side), [Find a co-founder](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-cofounder) when the move is into equity-level partnership.
