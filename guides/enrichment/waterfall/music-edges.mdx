The Music Industry Waterfall uses the canonical graph edge definitions in [Ontology → MusicBrainz Edges](/guides/ontology/edges#musicbrainz). This page is the **pipeline-scoped catalog**: which edges this pipeline emits, at what weight, from which writer. Edge patterns, properties, examples, and description rules should be edited only in the ontology source of truth.

<Note>
  Ground truth: [MusicBrainz Edges](/guides/ontology/edges#musicbrainz) \+ [Edge Weights](/guides/ontology/edge-weights) (**lower = stronger**; hub-node edges sit Weak or below; never sub-1). **`Artist`** in a pattern means `Person:Music_Artist` or `Music_Group`; `MEMBER_OF` is always `(Person:Music_Artist)→(Music_Group)`.
</Note>

## Standard edge envelope

Canonical section: [Standard Music Edge Envelope](/guides/ontology/edges#music-standard-edge-envelope). Every edge stamps `uuid`, `weight`, `created_at` / `updated_at` via `timestamp()`, and semantic-null `description` / `description_generated_at` on create (the describers fill them later — see [Edge descriptions](#edge-descriptions)).

## Membership & artist ↔ artist

Canonical section: [Membership & Artist Relationships](/guides/ontology/edges#music-membership-artist-relationships).

- `MEMBER_OF` `(Person:Music_Artist)→(Music_Group)` · weight **5** current / **12** past · props `instruments`, `begin`, `end`, `ended`, `original` — written by the artist orchestrator (#13094) from `member of band` rels.
- `COLLABORATED_WITH` (w15), `SUPPORTING_MUSICIAN_FOR` (w18), `TRIBUTE_TO` (w40) — **design entries, no live writer** (allowlisted for descriptions but unwired; collaborators deferred to v2).

## Creative credits

Canonical section: [Creative Credits](/guides/ontology/edges#music-creative-credits). The heart of the music graph. `PLAYED_ON` / `PRODUCED` / `ENGINEERED` are **type-aware** — they can originate from `Music_Group` as well as `Person:Music_Artist` (#13100 splits by MusicBrainz `artist.type`).

## Structural music (hub-aware)

Canonical section: [Structural Music and Labels](/guides/ontology/edges#music-structural-labels). Includes the **forward-derived** `ON_RECORD_LABEL` (#13131, no MusicBrainz fetch — a one-hop shortcut of `APPEARS_ON → RELEASED_ON` sourced from the canonical release, called by #13099 with `release_mbid` scope).

## Events & Geography

Canonical section: [Events, Places & Geography](/guides/ontology/edges#music-events-places-geography). `FROM_AREA` (w70) is a **forward-path gap** — graph-derived outside the audited ingest path while sweeps are excluded.

## Identity & ownership bridges

Canonical section: [Identity & Ownership Bridges](/guides/ontology/edges#music-identity-ownership-bridges).

- `SAME_AS` — identity weight **1** (fixed-forward): `(Company:Music_Label)→(Company)` from the depth-0 label orchestrator (#13091) and the reverse bridge's `link-label-to-company` (#13172); `(Person:Music_Artist)→(Person/master_person)` from person-resolution (#13121) and the reverse bridge's `link-artist-to-person` (#13170).
- `OWNED_BY` `(Company:Music_Label)→(Company)` · weight **15** · props `match_method`, `confidence`, `source` — written by `mb/resolve-and-link-label-owner` (#13133): MusicBrainz label-relation parent first (Tier 1), Exa/LLM owner discovery as fallback (Tier 2 via #13132).
- `ACQUIRED_BY` `(Company:Music_Label)→(Company)` · weight **25** — written by #13133 when acquisition evidence is found (MusicBrainz relations or Exa acquisition-announcement extraction), carrying `acquisition_date` / `acquisition_year`. **Coexists with `OWNED_BY`** — when no independent owner resolves, the acquirer also becomes the current owner.

## Expertise (credit-derived)

`HAS_EXPERTISE` `(Person)→(DomainExpertise / SubDomainExpertise)` — bridged music people get expertise edges straight from their MusicBrainz credits via the deterministic emitter `mb-credits-to-expertise` (#13160, no LLM), driven by the `mb_role_expertise_map` table (#755): per-mapping weight \+ confidence, `signal_type: mb_credit`, `expertise_strength` from credit depth/volume, `person_has_expertise` (#711) dual-write. Runs from person-resolution (#13121 v2.7) after each successful `SAME_AS` bridge.

## Edge descriptions

Canonical section: [Music Edge Descriptions](/guides/ontology/edges#music-edge-descriptions). One generic trio covers **all** music edge types, keyed on `e.uuid` (unlike IMDB's per-type trio keyed on endpoint UUIDs): create #13095 → write #13096, driven in-band by #13158 from every live edge writer, with async fallback #13097 (Task 181, sandbox). Model `deepseek/deepseek-v4-flash`, GOOD-or-null invariant. Detail: [Music Waterfall Functions → LLM edge descriptions](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/music-functions#llm-edge-descriptions).
