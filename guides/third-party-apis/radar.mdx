## What it is

[Radar](https://radar.com) is a location infrastructure platform providing geocoding, geofencing, trip tracking, and address verification APIs. It offers both forward and reverse geocoding, IP-based geolocation, and address autocomplete.

## Why we use it

- **Geocoding** — convert addresses to coordinates and vice versa
- **Address verification** — validate and standardize addresses
- **IP geolocation** — determine user location from IP for timezone and region detection
- **Geofencing** — location-based triggers and region detection

## How we use it at Orbiter

Radar provides geocoding and location services for resolving company and person addresses into structured location data (city, region, country) that feeds into the graph as LOCATED_IN, IN_REGION, and IN_COUNTRY edges.
