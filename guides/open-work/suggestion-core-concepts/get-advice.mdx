## Description

This outcome type is the catch-all for off-pattern requests that don't fit cleanly into the other 13. The User has a specific question or situation and needs to find someone with relevant expertise — usually a one-time conversation, not an ongoing relationship. Personal asks ("I want to lose 20 pounds") and tactical professional questions ("I need an M&A tax attorney") both land here when no other outcome type fits.

**Best for:** off-pattern user asks, niche expert one-off conversations, personal/lifestyle questions outside the standard business outcomes.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find a mentor / advisor](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-mentor-advisor) when the relationship is ongoing instead of one-off, [Make a purchase](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/make-a-purchase) when the relationship is a paid service rather than a conversation.

## Example Outcomes

Synthesized examples of what a User might create in the AI outcome chat for this type:

### Example 1 — Personal health goal

<div style={{padding: '16px', border: '2px solid #6B9FE8', borderRadius: '8px', color: '#fff'}}>

I want to lose 20 pounds in the next 6 months without crash dieting. Looking for a few people I can talk to — ideally a registered dietitian who works with high-stress executives, plus maybe someone in sports medicine. Not interested in influencer-style coaches or generic personal trainers; want clinically credible people.

</div>

### Example 2 — Tactical legal question

<div style={{padding: '16px', border: '2px solid #6B9FE8', borderRadius: '8px', color: '#fff'}}>

Founder, just signed an LOI on an acquisition and ran into a tax structuring issue with rollover equity. Need to talk to an M&A tax attorney who's specifically handled founder rollover structures in the last 24 months. One conversation, not an ongoing engagement — already have outside counsel for the broader deal.

</div>

### Example 3 — Career direction conversation

<div style={{padding: '16px', border: '2px solid #6B9FE8', borderRadius: '8px', color: '#fff'}}>

Senior engineer at a public tech company, considering leaving to do a PhD in AI safety. Want to talk to 3–5 people who actually made that transition (not just considered it) — engineers who left high-paying industry roles for graduate research. Specifically want to hear from people 2–4 years post-decision so they have honest perspective.

</div>
