## Description

This outcome type surfaces people for ongoing guidance roles — formal (advisor with equity, board member, board chair) or informal (mentor). The relationship is recurring and typically organized around a domain of expertise the User wants regular access to.

**Best for:** founders building an advisory bench, leaders looking for outside perspective, executives forming boards.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Get advice / consultation](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/get-advice) when the ask is one-off instead of ongoing, [Find a co-founder](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-cofounder) when the commitment is full-time partnership.
