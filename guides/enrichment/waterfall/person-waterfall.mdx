Get-add-driven person enrichment with a **tiered provider cascade** (PDL → Enrich Layer → Exa Fiber → Exa, plus an async FullEnrich tier and the local Fundable mirror), a **controlled, non-recursive waterfall**, a single priority-tiered queue, and upsert dedup. This page is the architecture spec: the source contract, depth model, tiers, queue semantics, the three-stage entry gate, the phase orchestrator, the controlled-waterfall model, seed flows, and a full cascade example. The entry-gate walkthrough lives on [Person Waterfall Entry Points](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-entry-points); the live function inventory, table schemas, and graph node \+ edge references live on the companion pages below.

The person waterfall is the **oldest and richest** enrichment pipeline — everything the [Company](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/company-waterfall), [IMDB](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/imdb-waterfall), and [Music](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/music-waterfall) waterfalls share was extracted from it. Like the company waterfall, everything enters through a synchronous **get-add entry gate** (`mvp/get-add/master-person-new` #13039) that dedups, sources, and creates inline — seeds dispatch the orchestrator immediately (async), while the queue holds leaves discovered at depth \> 0. It differs structurally in three ways: the provider layer is a **fallback ladder** (each tier fires only when the previous one came back thin), one provider tier is **asynchronous** (FullEnrich returns via webhook after the gate has exited), and company enrichment from a person run is **selective by design** — under the 2026-07-04/05 controlled-waterfall policy a root run full-enriches its **selected current company** and its **schools**, queues **past employers** as depth-1 leaf companies, and links everything else lookup-/local-only. See [Core Concepts](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/core-concepts) for shared mechanics (cascade depth, priority tiers, queue tables).

## Source contract — PDL → Enrich Layer → Exa Fiber → Exa (\+ FullEnrich \+ Fundable)

Unlike the company waterfall's parallel provider fan-in, the person providers form a **fallback ladder**: PDL is the cheapest first call, Enrich Layer fires only when PDL comes back thin (404 **or** 200 with empty `experience`), the Exa Fiber verified-email fallback fires only when EL surfaced no usable LinkedIn and both an email \+ `full_name` exist, Exa fires only when PDL is still thin after the derived-LinkedIn retry, and FullEnrich is the asynchronous last resort for email-only seeds. The natural keys are **profile URLs** in `master_link` (lowercased \+ normalized, per owner), **emails**, name-guarded **phones**, and the direct `master_person.linkedin_url` fallback — walked in that order by `match-master-person` #13038. There is no unique-index backstop — dedup is the app-level ladder (pre-API local dedup \+ the 4-tier match).

## Depth model

The person waterfall is non-recursive by construction — depth only ever increases, and fanout privileges shrink with it.

| Depth | Meaning | Behavior |
| :-: | --- | --- |
| **0** | Seed (user / smoke / direct) | Full pipeline: the synchronous PDL → EL → Fiber → Exa cascade \+ FullEnrich at the gate, then all 13 phases with seed privileges — LLM bios, optional deep research, six-resolver edge resolution, expertise, investor overlay, direct angel portfolio edges (default-on). The **selected current company full-enriches** (inline at phase 3 \+ the orchestrator's sparse-check exit dispatch), **schools full-enrich inline**, and **past employers queue as depth-1 leaf companies** |
| **1** | Leaf (queued discoveries; Exa C-suite direct dispatches) | The gate **skips the external cascade** — the person is created from passed data \+ Fundable and queued (or, for C-suite, direct-dispatched in `current_company_only` mode). A drained depth-1 run still fully enriches the person (Phase 0 PDL backfill \+ Phase 1 EL), and its schools still full-enrich inline — but company C-suite/founder expansion is depth-0-gated on the company side, so no leaf company can start a new people wave |
| **2** | Leaf-of-leaf company rows | Company leaves queued from a depth-1 person's history sit behind the same depth gates; they enrich as companies without people expansion. The waterfall cannot recurse into another person wave at any depth |

<Check>
  **Why non-recursive?** One seed person's PDL work history alone exposes 5–10 employers and schools, and an angel seed adds an investment network — portfolio companies, co-angels, VC firms, partners. The controls: company **C-suite/founder expansion is depth-0-gated** (#12992 v4.2/v4.3), C-suite people run `current_company_only` with broad edge resolution suppressed, person-to-person fanout was removed from phase 9 entirely (direct portfolio edges replaced the co-investor cascade), and the drain cron ships inactive with a tier ≤ 1 gate.
</Check>

## Priority tiers

### queue\_enrich\_person (1 = highest)

`queue_enrich_person` (table #582) uses the shared tier vocabulary — `1` founders / current-network people · `2` past-network / partner-adjacent · `3` schools / angels · `4` everything else, **4 = default**. Live writers:

| Tier | Who queues it | Live status |
| :-: | --- | --- |
| **1** | Co-angels \+ VC partners from `resolve-investors-edges` #12702 — driven by the **company-side** deal cascade (#12703 → #12856) | live |
| **1** | YC founders / partner from the company side (`process-yc-people` #12700, seed-company only) | live |
| **1** | Operator / backfill seeds at depth 0 — empty-PDL backfill (Task #156), divergent-LP seeding (Task #179) | tasks ship inactive |
| **2** | Fundable smoke seeds (sandbox Task #185, via #13039 at depth 0 / tier 2) | sandbox |
| — | Exa C-suite / founder people | **NOT queued** — direct async dispatch of #13040 with `current_company_only: true` (no queue row) |
| **4** | Any gate queue path without an explicit tier | default |

### What a root person run fans out

```text
seed person (depth 0)
  selected current company     → FULL ENRICH (phase 3 resolves + links; the
                                 orchestrator exit dispatches enrich-master-company
                                 at depth 0 when the company is still sparse)
  schools                      → FULL ENRICH inline in resolve-edges-education
                                 (cascade_depth 1 — C-suite expansion stays gated)
  past / non-primary employers → company shells + queue_enrich_company (depth 1 leaves)
  cert/honor issuers, publishers,
  volunteer orgs               → lookup-/local-only links, never queued
  angel portfolio companies    → direct (Person)-[:INVESTED_IN w25]->(Company) edges
                                 (default-on at depth 0; no co-investor fanout)
  Exa C-suite people (company-seed lane)
      → direct async dispatch {depth effective+1, current_company_only: true}
        — never queued
```

### Upsert on duplicate

When the same `master_person_id` is enqueued twice, `upsert-enrich-person` #12854 (v1.1 — the **single sanctioned writer** for all `queue_enrich_person` rows; the already-queued branch lives inside it):

- `count++` on every duplicate request (a request tally, not an attempt counter)
- `priority_tier` — only ever improves (min of existing and incoming)
- `cascade_depth` — only ever improves (min); a later seed-level request promotes a leaf row
- `deep_research` — **OR-promoted**: once any request asks for deep research, the row keeps it
- One row per person enforced in application code only — there is **no unique index** on `queue_enrich_person.master_person_id`

## Queue & upsert semantics

- **Natural key is `master_person_id`** — the person row always exists _before_ it is queued (the entry gate creates it first).
- **Sync-enrich branch:** `cascade_depth == 0 && queue == false && !hold_downstream` → dispatch #13040 async-shared immediately, no queue row.
- **Forced queue at depth \> 0:** `queue || cascade_depth > 0` always routes to the queue upsert — a depth-1 discovery can never trigger inline full enrichment, and the gate skips the paid provider cascade for it entirely.
- **Governor flags on the entry gate:** `hold_downstream` (no dispatch, no queue row, `qa_passed` skip logged — the interactive-QA mode), `orbiter_verified` (marks user-supplied input emails only), and the v1.10 `suppress_downstream` pass-through to `create-master-link` on all contained paths.
- **Drain layer:** Task #126 (cron, **`active = false` live**) picks **one `priority_tier <= 1` row** per 600 s tick — sorted `priority_tier asc, cascade_depth asc, id asc` — locks it, runs #13040 with the stored `master_person_id` / `deep_research` / `cascade_depth`, deletes on success, releases \+ `log_crash` on failure. Operator batch drain: `process-enrichment-queue` #12816 (v1.2 — deterministic Xano-native selection, `min_count` defaults 0, routes through the gated orchestrators so depth gates apply), or Run Now on #126. Cron detail: [Person Waterfall Crons](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-crons).
- **Index gaps (app-level integrity):** `queue_enrich_person` has no unique key, `master_person` has no `linkedin_url` / `node_uuid` index, and `person_enrich_data` has no `master_person_id` index — dedup and idempotency live in #13038 / #12854, not the DB. Field-by-field schemas: [Person Waterfall Tables](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-tables).

## Pipeline architecture — the three-stage entry gate

```text
mvp/get-add/master-person-new — #13039
```

The entry gate splits the retired monolith into three composable stages, then dispatches the orchestrator:

```text
1 · cascade-person-data #13037 v1.9   PDL → Enrich Layer → Exa Fiber → PDL retry → Exa
2 · match-master-person #13038 v1.0   4-tier id resolution + phone format
3 · master-person-new #13039 v1.21    kill switch · pre-API local dedup · Fundable sync ·
                                      contact write loops · add-person-node · node_registry ·
                                      dispatch
      → enrich-master-person #13040 v3.33 (async-shared orchestrator)
```

| Stage | Function | Responsibility |
| :-: | --- | --- |
| 1 | `mvp/enrich/cascade-person-data` #13037 (v1.9) | Pure data fetch, in order: PDL → Enrich Layer (email #12734 / LinkedIn #12612) → Exa Fiber verified-email fallback #13179 (promoted LinkedIn re-runs EL) → PDL retry via the derived LinkedIn URL → Exa (#12936). Returns the payloads plus deduped `el_links`, `input_email_source`, the audited `exa_fiber_attempt`, and a `needs_full_enrich` flag when sources are thin. No DB writes except `log_crash` on a caught exception. |
| 2 | `mvp/resolve/match-master-person` #13038 (v1.0) | Resolves the `master_person` id: `master_link.link_url` → `master_email.email_address` → name-guarded `master_phone.phone_number` (with phone-conflict detection) → direct `master_person.linkedin_url` fallback. Formats every phone via `llm-phone-format` #2540 and returns `match_via` \+ the formatted list for caller reuse. No graph writes. |
| 3 | `mvp/get-add/master-person-new` #13039 (v1.21) | Kill switch #4617 → **pre-API local dedup** (links / emails / `linkedin_url` — skips the paid cascade on repeat lookups) → cascade → match → Fundable lookup from the enriched link set → contact write loops (#2784 / #2541 / #2783) → Fundable direct-field sync \+ display-name repair → `add-person-node` #2601 (graph `Person` node) \+ `node_registry` stamp (#13183, v1.21) → downstream dispatch. When `needs_full_enrich`, also fires FullEnrich #13036 async. Every path returns an explicit `person_created` flag (v1.20). |

**Downstream dispatch.** When the person is new and not queued (`cascade_depth == 0 && queue == false && !hold_downstream`), #13039 fires **`enrich-master-person` #13040** in **async-shared** mode (`master_person_id`, `deep_research`, `cascade_depth`). Otherwise it routes through `upsert-enrich-person` #12854. `hold_downstream` skips both paths and logs a `qa_passed` skip row — and it also passes `suppress_downstream: true` on every `create-master-link` call (as does any `cascade_depth > 0` request).

Full calling contract and section-by-section walkthrough: [Person Waterfall Entry Points](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-entry-points). The public HTTP entry is **`POST /master-persons`** — endpoint #7977 (V2 Master Persons, group 350, `api:WKgay2AU`) → #13039.

## The controlled waterfall

**The 2026-07-04/05 policy is the defining fact of the current pipeline.** The June containment sweep (zero company writes from person runs) proved too tight — current employers and schools settled sparse. The controlled waterfall re-opens exactly three lanes, each bounded:

| Lane | Mechanism | Bound |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Selected current company → FULL ENRICH** | Phase 3 (#12824 v3.5) resolves the best current employer through the company gate on root runs; the orchestrator exit (v3.31–v3.33) re-checks the linked company and direct-dispatches `enrich-master-company` at depth 0 when it is still sparse (`about_500` or `last_enrich` empty), then clears its queue residue | One company per person; C-suite people-wave inside that company run is depth-0-gated on the company side and its people run `current_company_only` |
| **Schools → FULL ENRICH inline** | `resolve-edges-education` #12560 (v1.9) creates the school local-only, then calls `enrich-master-company` inline at `cascade_depth: 1`; v1.10–v1.13 add strong school-evidence gates, name reuse, a DeepSeek same-school tie-breaker, and weak-row suppression | Depth 1 — no C-suite expansion; evidence gates stop biography noise from minting schools |
| **Past / non-primary employers → depth-1 leaf queue** | `resolve-edges-work` #12562 (v1.8+) and phase 4b #13031 (v1.8/v1.9) mint company shells and upsert `queue_enrich_company` rows; placeholder orgs ("Stealth Startup", "Angel Investor") are blocked | Queued leaves drain as ordinary company enrichment behind the company tier gates — no people expansion at depth \> 0 |

Everything else stays contained: cert/honor issuers, publishers, and volunteer orgs are lookup-/local-only leaves; `create-master-link` #2541 suppresses all 9 scraper dispatch sites on contained paths; and `scrape-twitter-person` #2106 treats expanded URLs as person-link evidence only.

### Company-seed C-suite mode (current\_company\_only)

The reverse lane — a **company** seed creating people — stays tightly contained. When `enrich-master-company` #12992 (v4.24) Step 7 accepts Exa C-suite/founder matches for a seed company (**depth-0-gated**, non-recursive; since v4.21 it can also synthesize verified founder matches from Company\_LLM / Fundable / LinkedIn evidence), each match goes through `master-person-from-exa` #12997 (v2.5): normalized-LinkedIn \+ name-fallback dedup, kill-switch-gated create, `is_c_suite: true`, new leaves start `visibility: false`, employer resolution lookup-only, a guaranteed `person_enrich_data` shell with the Exa payload stashed, an immediate EL `person-linkedin` profile fetch, and ScrapeCreators employee-card reconciliation (canonical LinkedIn \+ avatar). Then — instead of a queue row — a direct async dispatch of #13040:

```json
{
  "cascade_depth": 1,
  "deep_research": false,
  "current_company_only": true
}
```

In-mode, the run is full for the person but narrow on expansion: PDL / Enrich Layer / Exa / FullEnrich all filter employment to **current roles**; Phase 1 skips the paid EL lookup for card-ready Exa leaves; Phase 7 runs **education only** (#12828 v1.3 — schools full-enrich even for C-suite leaves); and the selected current company may materialize on strong Exa evidence but stays `hold_downstream`. Work rows \+ `WORKS_AT`-family edges materialize inside the dispatched run (phase 3 PDL \+ phase 4b Exa; edges via #2800 — there is **no `HAS_WORKED_AT`** edge type). Company-born people cannot spawn a new graph wave.

### Direct angel portfolio edges (the 2026-07-02 D-series)

Phase 9 no longer cascades deals from the person side. For a depth-0 angel, each `fundable_angel_investments` row becomes a **direct `(Person:Angel)-[:INVESTED_IN w25]->(Company)` edge** MERGEd against the canonical `master_company_node_uuid` (ON CREATE stamps a self-healing stub; skipped only when no company node uuid exists — never an orphan), with an LLM-or-deterministic description. **The gate defaults ON** (`allow_investor_network_cascade|first_notnull:true && cascade_depth == 0`); pass `false` to suppress. No `Funding_Round` materialization, no co-investor/VC/partner fanout, and **no queue writes** happen on this path — the full deal cascade (`Funding_Round` \+ `RAISED` \+ the investor edge set \+ co-angel/partner queueing at depth 1 / tier 1) runs from the **company side** (#12703 → #12856 v1.10 → #12702 v1.17).

## Loop prevention (ten layers)

1. **Pre-API local dedup \+ the 4-tier match ladder** (#13039 / #13038) — an existing person is returned and re-attached, never re-created.
2. **Queue upsert** #12854 — one row per person (app-level), `count++`, min tier, min depth, OR-promoted `deep_research`.
3. **Depth-0 gate on company C-suite expansion** (#12992 v4.2/v4.3) — only seed companies mint people, and those people run `current_company_only`.
4. **`current_company_only`** — the C-suite lane filters employment to current roles and runs education-only edge resolution (#12828 v1.3).
5. **Direct angel edges instead of deal fanout** — phase 9 writes portfolio `INVESTED_IN` edges; co-investor/partner expansion lives only on the company side, behind its own gates.
6. **Leaf queueing, not inline enrichment** — past employers become `queue_enrich_company` rows (depth 1) that drain behind the company tier gates; placeholder orgs are blocked outright.
7. **`suppress_downstream`** — all 9 async scraper dispatch sites in `create-master-link` suppressed on contained paths (#2541 \+ #13039 v1.10).
8. **Stale-dispatch guard \+ 60 s source-keyed debounce** on the orchestrator (`force_run` bypass is deliberate — used by the FullEnrich re-run).
9. **Kill switch** `check-kill-switch-v2` #4617 — `master_person` row-count cap (the `kill_switch_person` row in `environment_variables` #272); fail-open via `log_crash`; when ON, existing people return local-only and blocked creations are audited in `log_crash` #542 only.
10. **Cron single-row tick** — Task #126 processes at most one tier ≤ 1 queue row per 600 s, and it ships `active = false`: the manual-drain state is itself the hardest stop.

## Implementation notes captured during build

- **`set-person-names` #12820 exists to dodge a Xano naming-collision bug** — `db.edit` with data keys named `first_name` / `last_name` silently resolves them to the parent function's empty inputs; the helper's `fn` / `ln` / `mn` / `sfx` / `nick` inputs are the single writer for name fields. **Xano artifact — do not reimplement.**
- **Phase 10 #12831 is a literal empty stub** (stack `{}`, response null since v2.0) — and #13040 v3.33 **still calls it**. Removing the call site is safe whenever someone next touches #13040.
- **Work-history titles no longer mint investment edges** (#2800 v2.5) — the `INVESTED_IN` w15 FOREACH is unreachable dead code; investment edges come from the deal writers only. The phantom-founder guard (v2.0) means a title-only "Founder at X" claim loses to Fundable's structured founder list.
- **FullEnrich data\_source threading is literal-dispatch:** webhook #8594 calls `db.set_datasource` with a literal `"live"` / `"staging"` / `"sandbox"` per branch (Xano won't accept a `$var` there), and its v1.8 guard **refuses any item with an empty `custom.data_source`** — the Carl→Robert live-corruption fix.
- **#12832's `db.query` where clause is stored as a backtick template string** with `&amp;&amp;` entities — the known inline-serializer artifact; flagged on [Person Waterfall Functions](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-functions). **Xano artifact — do not reimplement.**
- **Do not copy the stale `*80` weight comment** in #12925's input doc: the live `HAS_EXPERTISE` matched-edge weight is `min(round(10 + score × 160), 50)`, created nodes get flat 10, and `expertise_strength` is a separate 0–100 property — see [Person Edges](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-edges).
- **Integrity is app-level:** no unique key on the queue, no `linkedin_url` / `node_uuid` index on `master_person`, no `master_person_id` index on `person_enrich_data` — two calls passing the dedup ladder simultaneously WILL double-create. Index inventory: [Person Waterfall Tables](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/person-tables).

## Motivation

A single person exposes **companies** (current employer, past employers, schools, cert issuers, volunteer orgs, publishers) and — when the person is an angel — an **investment network** (portfolio companies, co-angels, VC firms, VC partners), each of whom exposes more rounds and more people. Without control, one LinkedIn URL would mint hundreds of entities and thousands of paid provider calls two hops out.

The person waterfall solves this the same way Company, IMDB, and Music do — **a get-or-create gate, a depth-capped non-recursive cascade, a priority-tiered queue, and upsert dedup** — with two structural twists: the provider layer is a **tiered fallback ladder** (pay for the next tier only when the previous one came back thin), and company enrichment from a person run is **selective**: the entities a card actually shows (current employer, schools) enrich fully, the long tail queues as leaves, and people expansion stays locked behind the company side's depth-0 gate.

**Goal:**

- From a seed **person**: create the canonical `master_person` \+ graph node, resolve contacts, avatar, LLM bios, expertise, and every edge family, fully enrich the current employer and schools, queue the rest of the work history as leaves, and build the investor profile with direct portfolio edges.
- From a seed **company** (the C-suite lane): enrich every Exa founder / C-suite match fully in `current_company_only` mode — current employer linked, education resolved, broad edge resolution skipped — without spawning another people or company wave.
