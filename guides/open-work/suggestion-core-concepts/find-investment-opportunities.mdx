## Description

This outcome type surfaces companies, deals, or assets for the User to invest in. The User is deploying capital — angel checks, VC follow-ons, family-office allocations, or PE/M&A platform plays — and wants targets that match a thesis.

**Best for:** angel investors, VC sourcing, PE/family-office deal flow, fund-of-funds allocators.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find investors](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-investors) for the inverse (raising capital), [Find acquisition targets](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-acquisition-target) for control deals instead of minority.

## Example Outcomes

Synthesized examples of what a User might create in the AI outcome chat for this type:

### Example 1 — Angel investor, AI infra thesis

<div style={{padding: '16px', border: '2px solid #6B9FE8', borderRadius: '8px', color: '#fff'}}>

I'm an angel writing \$25K–\$100K checks. Looking for pre-seed AI infrastructure companies — particularly inference optimization, model serving, or eval tooling. Want technical founders out of FAANG / top AI labs, not first-time non-technical CEOs. SF-based deals only, since I want to meet in person before checks.

### Example 2 — Family office, growth-stage consumer

<div style={{padding: '16px', border: '2px solid #6B9FE8', borderRadius: '8px', color: '#fff'}}>

Check size \$5M–\$15M as either lead or significant follow-on. Avoid anything heavy on celebrity/influencer-only distribution.

</div>

### Example 3 — PE associate, vertical SaaS roll-up

<div style={{padding: '16px', border: '2px solid #6B9FE8', borderRadius: '8px', color: '#fff'}}>

Associate at a lower-mid-market PE firm. Sourcing vertical SaaS targets for a roll-up thesis: sub-\$10M EBITDA, founder-owned, niche industries (specifically property management, dental, and accounting verticals). Need to find founders open to majority recapitalization, not just venture rounds.

</div>
