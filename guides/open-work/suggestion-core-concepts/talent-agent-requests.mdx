**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `rep_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `rep_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `direction` is the load-bearing routing field. `find_work_for_client` and `submit_on_opening`
  route to the buyer/role pipeline; `package_project` routes to the talent/credit-match
  pipeline; `map_access` routes to the warm-path graph.
- `confidentiality.tier` is non-optional. `embargoed` means do not surface to anyone outside
  the explicit allowlist. `soft` means signed-NDA contacts only. `public` means trade-press
  rumors are fair game.
- `director_spec.must_haves` (and the equivalent in other spec shapes — `actor_spec`,
  `writer_spec`, `showrunner_spec`) are filters — a candidate missing any one is excluded.
  `nice_to_haves` are ranking signals only.
- `submission_window.exclusivity_required_per_buyer_days` is how long a buyer holds before
  the project goes wider — score buyers on relationship strength because the first call matters.
- `access_map_requested.warm_paths_needed_to` is the most agent-shaped feature: surface people
  by graph distance with the agency's existing relationships, not just public credit overlap.
- `format_of_results` is one of `suggestion | csv | pdf`. `suggestion` returns enriched cards
  into the copilot UI; `csv`/`pdf` are operator-facing exports for internal agency routing.
- `exclusion_list.notes` and the "no-fly" list are enforced strictly — never surface flagged
  names regardless of all other scores.

## Description

This outcome request type is built for agents at a film & TV talent agency representing
clients across departments (literary, talent, motion picture, television, alternative,
below-the-line, packaging). The matching surface is multi-modal: it surfaces buyer execs,
packaging candidates, comparable deals, and warm-path contacts depending on which
direction the agent is moving.

**Best for:** packaging projects, submitting clients on openings, mapping access at a
specific buyer, finding directors / writers / actors to attach, and managing the deal
cycle around a client's quote, holds, and exclusivity windows.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Find collaborators](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-collaborators)
when the ask is creative pairing outside a packaged project, [Find speakers /
get speaking opportunities](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-speakers)
when a client is being booked for non-acting stage time (festivals, panels, awards-season
press), [Find media / PR / journalists](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-media-pr)
for awards-season campaigns and profile pieces.
