**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `partnership_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `partnership_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- Matching targets are companies (not individuals), but the result card should surface the
  specific `entry_point.target_titles` person inside that company.
- `partner_archetype.tech_stacks` is a hard filter for this outcome — a firm without Python or
  TypeScript delivery capability is excluded regardless of other signals.
- `value_exchange` is directional context, not a scoring dimension; use it to draft the
  outreach message after matching.
- `exclusion_list.company_domains` is populated when the user uploads an existing partner list
  or a competitor roster; deduplicate before scoring.
- `format_of_results: "suggestion"` returns enriched company + contact cards into the copilot
  UI. Use `csv` for a list the BD team can work from in a spreadsheet.

## Description

This outcome type surfaces companies (and the right person inside them) for partnership-level relationships — co-marketing, integrations, co-selling, channel deals, or joint go-to-market. The relationship is partner-to-partner, not vendor-to-customer or investor-to-portfolio.

**Best for:** BD/partnerships leads, founder-led channel building, ecosystem teams, joint-venture scoping.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Prospect for customers / clients](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/prospect-customers-clients) when selling to instead of partnering with, [Find acquisition targets](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-acquisition-target) when M&A is on the table.
