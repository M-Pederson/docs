## Credit edges

### WORKED\_ON

`(Person)-[:WORKED_ON]->(Film_TV)` ┬Ě weight **30**. Written for every credit whose film is already in the graph by `add-credit-edges-from-person` (#13013) and `add-credit-edges-from-title` (#13015), plus `#2334` in award context. Each side silently skips endpoints whose other side hasn't been enriched yet ÔÇö symmetric, so the edge appears once both exist. See [Cyphers Ôćĺ Film & TV](/guides/cyphers/film-tv).
