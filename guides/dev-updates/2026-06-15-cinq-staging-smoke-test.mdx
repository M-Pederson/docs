**Session (2026-06-15).** A real run of the [music waterfall](/guides/enrichment/waterfall/music-nodes) against **`X-Data-Source: staging`** (not sandbox) — the live business graph (~3,700 companies, ~2,600 funding rounds, ~620 people). Staging held **zero** music nodes beforehand, so everything here is net-new and cleanly separable (every node carries a music label / `mbid`). Doubles as the at-scale validation of the [forward-path Gemini embeddings](/guides/dev-updates/2026-06-15-gemini-embeddings-people-company) on staging.

<Note>
**Data left in place** as a live staging fixture (by request). The whole music subgraph is isolatable — a single `DETACH DELETE` over the music labels + the `cinqmusicgroup.com` bridge removes it if needed.
</Note>

## What ran

Label **Cinq Music Group LLC** (`fb742ab4-4035-4ef1-8684-98eaa80f6fe2`) + its top 6 artists by catalog presence, each enriched to up to 50 recordings.

**Method — bounded, not a catalog cascade.** Cinq is a large distributor (33 releases, ~36 artists on MB); an unbounded `seed/label` drain would pull its whole web into the live graph. So:

1. `seed/artist` ×6 (depth-0 synchronous) → 6 artist nodes + members via `MEMBER_OF`.
2. Per artist, browsed MusicBrainz for ≤50 recording MBIDs, then `seed/recording` each **directly** (depth-0, fully enriched) — **284 recordings**, serial at MusicBrainz's 1 req/s (~30 min). The unified queue was deliberately **not** drained, so Cinq's wider catalog did not expand.
3. `seed/label` → `Music_Label` node + `cinqmusicgroup.com` Company bridge.

Operator endpoints (group `musicbrainz`, canonical `P8PNYYKM`): `seed/artist` #8644, `seed/recording` #8666, `seed/label` #8643, `tool/run-cypher` #8665. Cron #180 ships inactive, so the run was driven manually.

## Embedding coverage — the validation

- **295 fully-enriched nodes** (6 seeds + 8 members + 280 recordings + label) → **100% carry both vectors**. The `name_embedding` count and the Gemini `embeddings` count are **both exactly 295** — i.e. zero nodes have one vector without the other. This is the proof the [dual-write](/guides/dev-updates/2026-06-15-gemini-embeddings-people-company) holds at volume on a real data source.
- **173 nodes carry no embeddings** — credit/cascade stubs: 136 collaborator `Music_Artist` + 29 `Music_Group` + 8 `Work`, MERGE-created as endpoints of credit edges, not enriched entities. Expected.

## Failures / notes

- 284 recordings attempted → **279 enriched, 4 `dedup_fresh`** (duplicate MBIDs correctly collapsed to 280 distinct `Recording` nodes), **1 true miss** (Daddy Yankee `e9f4a118…`, empty response — transient MB timeout, re-seedable).
- The cascade pulled in 165 collaborator artist/group stubs + 8 works **only** from the seeded recordings' own credits — bounded, since the queue was not drained.
- 7 recordings lack `length_ms` (MB coverage, not a pipeline fault). The 24 missing covers were **subsequently filled to 277/280 precise** by the [recording cover-art fallback](/guides/dev-updates/2026-06-15-recording-cover-art-fallback) (Deezer → iTunes), with 3 obscure tracks left correctly imageless.
