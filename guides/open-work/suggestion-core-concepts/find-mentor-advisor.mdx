**Notes for downstream agents**

- `outcome_summary` is the human-readable one-liner; `advisor_spec` is the structured truth.
  Score on `advisor_spec`, render on `outcome_summary`.
- `must_haves` are hard filters — a candidate missing any one is excluded. `nice_to_haves` are
  ranking signals only.
- `engagement_model` and `seat_type` determine what equity and time parameters to apply when
  scoring; a `board_member` candidate should have verifiable prior board service, not just advisory
  credits.
- `connection_appetite.cold_intro_acceptable: false` means only surface candidates within 2 hops
  of the user's network or with a mutual warm path.
- `exclusion_list.existing_board_members` is a freeform conflict note — parse for known firm names
  and exclude anyone employed by or significantly affiliated with those firms.

## Description

This outcome type surfaces people for ongoing guidance roles — formal (advisor with equity, board member, board chair) or informal (mentor). The relationship is recurring and typically organized around a domain of expertise the User wants regular access to.

**Best for:** founders building an advisory bench, leaders looking for outside perspective, executives forming boards.

**Adjacent outcomes:** [Get advice / consultation](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/get-advice) when the ask is one-off instead of ongoing, [Find a co-founder](/guides/open-work/suggestion-core-concepts/find-cofounder) when the commitment is full-time partnership.
